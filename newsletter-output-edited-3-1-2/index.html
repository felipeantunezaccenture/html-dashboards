2b. Claude Skills & Team Repository 🛠️
✦
What is a Claude Skill?
Requires Claude Code (Desktop)

Skills are very specific instructions written in Markdown. Claude reads them every time you call the Skill. Write it once, call it forever with /skill-name.

💡
The rule of thumb
If you've typed the same instructions more than twice → it's a Skill.
Markdown: the language between you and Claude

Claude reads Markdown in your prompts and Skill files, and writes it in every response. The better your Markdown, the more precise the output.

Claude reads it
In prompts & Skill files
Any Markdown in a prompt or SKILL.md is interpreted as structure instructions.
Claude writes it
In every response
That's the ## and **bold** you see. Specify format and Claude follows it.
Skill file structure & best practices
---
name: meeting-notes
description: "When to activate"
---

## When to use
Trigger condition

## Output format
Attendees · Decisions · Actions

## Rules
- Rule 1 (be specific)
- Rule 2

## Example output
Paste exactly what you want
📏
Keep it under 200 lines
Claude reads the full file on every call. Under 200 lines keeps it sharp.
🎯
Be specific in "Output format"
Vague instructions → vague output. Name every header, bullet style, and field.
📎
Include an example output
One good example outperforms three paragraphs of instructions.
⭐
Name it for how you'll call it
/meeting-notes beats /my-note-formatter-v2.
📂
Skills already available for the team — from our GitHub repository (/accenture-brand, /credential-generator, /business-case, /data-governance…) and Claude built-ins (/clear, /compact, /memory…).

Carús

🍽️ Loud Table Club

Exactly what a casa de comidas should be — unfussy, generous, honest. Fabada, verdinas con almejas, cachopo, arroz con leche. Large room, terrace when the sun cooperates, prices that stay real. Come with people who eat.

★★★★★
Bar & Aperitivo

Masa

🍷 Just One (Lie)

All-natural wines from family-run and women-led producers, rotating constantly. The food is short and surprising — ceviche tostadas, Thai chicken sandos. Vinyl on the speakers, terrace when the sun's out. Come for one glass. You know how this ends.

★★★★★
Date Night

El Nacional

❤️ Low Lights, High Stakes

A one-of-a-kind space in the heart of Passeig de Gràcia — four restaurants and four bars under one modernist roof. Too many options not to stay all night. Perfect for a date when you want to impress without having to commit to just one place.

★★★★★
Friends & Family

Els Pescadors

🍽️ Loud Table Club

A temple of high-end seafood cooking in Barcelona — fresh, local, and done with real care. The kind of place where the table fills up fast and nobody's in a hurry to leave. Order the rice, share everything, and let the meal take its time.

★★★★★
Bar & Aperitivo

El Velódromo

🍷 Just One (Lie)

A Barcelona institution on Carrer Muntaner — classic tapas, legendary croquetas, and a gastronomy overseen by Michelin-starred chef Jordi Vilà. Open all day, good at any hour. You come for one drink and end up ordering half the menu. No regrets.
